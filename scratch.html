Country Music

The sound of the South, the soul of America.

Why Country Music?

Country music has always felt like home to me. Whether it's the storytelling of classic artists or the energy of modern country, there's something about this genre that connects people in a way few others can. From road trips to Friday nights, country has been the soundtrack to some of my best memories.

The Grand Ole Opry — Nashville, Tennessee. Home of country music since 1925.

My Top Country Artists

These are the artists that defined my love for the genre, ranked in order of how much I've had them on repeat:

  1. Morgan Wallen
    • Dangerous: The Double Album is one of the most complete country records ever made
    • His voice has a raw, honest quality that's impossible to fake
    • Brought a younger generation to country music
  2. Luke Combs
    • Known for massive anthems like "When It Rains It Pours" and "Beautiful Crazy"
    • Consistently delivers emotional, relatable storytelling
  3. Chris Stapleton
    • Arguably the best pure vocalist in country music today
    • Blends country, blues, and soul in a way nobody else does
  4. Zach Bryan
    • Started posting acoustic videos from a Navy base and blew up overnight
    • "Something in the Orange" is a modern classic
  5. George Strait
    • The King of Country — 60 number-one hits speak for themselves
    • Proof that traditional country will never go out of style

Can't Miss Track
